About the Website

This website is an educational hub for understanding computer networks, data transmission, and the evolution of digital communication platforms. It combines clear explanations of networking fundamentals with practical examples of hardware, protocols, security, and a timeline of how social media emerged.

What You Can Explore

The site is structured into dedicated pages so readers can explore each topic step-by-step. The Introduction & Concepts page covers the core ideas behind computer networks and social networks, including how communication systems connect devices and people. The Hardware page presents physical networking equipment such as routers, switches, cables, and servers. The Transmission page explains how data moves over wired and wireless media, including copper, fiber, and radio links. The Topologies page explores common network layouts and how devices are arranged to share information efficiently. The Data & Protocols page describes how data is processed, formatted, and controlled using communication rules and standards. The Security page covers essential protections, threats, and practices for keeping networks and data safe. The Timeline page shows a historical view of the Internet and social media milestones, explaining how modern digital communication evolved.

This page also includes the same overview information found in the homepage about section, making it easy to learn who created the project and why.
